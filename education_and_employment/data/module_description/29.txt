Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC): Metropolis-Hastings (MH) and Gibbs sampler This course will provide the underlying theory for Bayesian statistics. Students will understand the role of the prior distribution and be able to formulate a posterior distribution (up to proportionality). They will also be exposed to the ideas of summarising posterior distributions and associated statistics. The underlying theory of Markov chain Monte Carlo will be introduced (Metropolis-Hastings and Gibbs sampler) with associated issues of implementation. None
