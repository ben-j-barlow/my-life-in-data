1. Basic principles of applied Bayesian analyses and reproducibility.2. Introduction to probabilistic programming packages.3. Generalised linear models with applications to real data using probabilistic programming packages.4. Mixed effects models. The course will provide the students with practical experience of applying Bayesian analyses to a range of statistical models. The statistical analyses will be conducted using the widely used computer package JAGS. An introduction to JAGS will be provided with additional hands-on experience. Assessment will be by written reports of Bayesian data analyses. None
