# Life Done Differently: Pieter Levels

- Had desire to escape the system. Didn’t want a 9-5. College was amazingly creative with everyone doing different things with their time. This ended when everyone got jobs, and they decided they had to party hard 2 days a week to make the other 5 worth it. Hated the system in terms of friendship dynamics and corporate dynamics.
- If you move to the other side of the world, you can reinvent yourself. You can make new friends similar to the person you want to become; new friends selected to be different from your old friends. 
- Aims to do IRL stuff. No screens. Get out in the real world.
- Mathematically, you could be better off putting all your money into Vanguard ETFs rather than buying a house. He has a laptop and a backpack. No need for a house.
- Believes in radical honesty; to himself and in his relationships.
