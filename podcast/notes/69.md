# Huberman: Leverage Dopamine

- Drop below baseline increases desire for more dopamine. 
- Distance and duration of dopamine peak makes the drop below dopamine baseline greater and last longer, e.g., cocaine 
- Sleep so critical to reset to dopamine levels (NSDR can help too)
- Increase baseline levels of dopamine is cold water therapy. Don’t do within 6 hours of strength training as it will block the good effect
- L-tyrosine 250-500mg prior to cognitive or physical work taken early in the day will achieve long lasting increases to dopamine baseline
- Procrastination:
- Dopamine is peaks and troughs. Troughs are pain experienced as cravings. Key to getting out of pain trough: (1) wait, procrastinate (2) 
- If you’re feeling amotivated, out your body and mind into a state of discomfort quickly. Engage in an unrelated activity (not related to your goal, eg, cold shower). The pain causes a rebound in dopamine. Do something really bad to steepen the trough and bring you back to baseline asap. Meditating is a poor solution because it’s so effortful to get into a meditative state. Make a short list of 5 effortful activities that are painful, easy to do, and are unrelated to your productivity goals.
